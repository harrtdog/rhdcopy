GALA SA PILIPINAS

About the Game

GALA SA PILIPINAS is an adventure game designed to preserve and promote Filipino culture. The game allows players to explore various provinces of the Philippines while overcoming challenges like the traditional Filipino Jeepney, all while learning about the nation's rich culture, history, and landmarks.

Game Features:

  • Progressive storytelling through different locations (Mt. Taal, Palawan, and Chocolate hills.)
  • Overcome the challenge of the jeepney, a beloved but sometimes troublesome traditional Filipino mode of transport.
  • Power-ups that enhance gameplay (e.g., Balut power-up allows flying ability)
  • Health bar system and scoring system

About the Character:

Angel is an adventurous young woman with a deep curiosity about the Philippines. She wants to travel the archipelago, seeking to experience its vibrant culture and explore its diverse regions. However, her journey isn't without obstacles. As she ventures through province, she encounters the traditional jeepney, a colorful and unique form of transportation that often blocks her path. The jeepney represents both the charm and challenge of Filipino culture. Along the way, she must navigate these challenges and solve problems to continue her adventure.
